Detailed Installation Guides

Learn exactly how to install and add mods to the three best alternative clients available today.

üì± Mojo Launcher (Mobile)

Play actual Java Edition on your phone.

  1. Download Mojo from the Play Store.
  2. Log in with your Microsoft account.
  3. Go to Settings > Java Tweaks and allocate exactly 50% of your phone's total RAM.
  4. To add mods: Open the app files via your file manager (`Android/data/git.artdeell.mojo/files/.minecraft/mods`).
  5. Drop your `.jar` files (like Fabric API and Sodium) into that folder.

üíª SK Launcher (PC)

The cleanest, offline-friendly launcher.

  1. Download SK Launcher from their official site.
  2. Go to the 'Installations' tab and click 'New'.
  3. Under 'Version', scroll to find 'Fabric' or 'Forge' for 1.21.11 and install it.
  4. Press Win + R, type %appdata%/.minecraft/mods.
  5. Drag your downloaded Modrinth `.jar` files into this folder and launch!

‚öîÔ∏è CM Client (PvP PC)

A built-in competitive PvP client.

  1. CM Client comes pre-packaged with essential PvP mods (Keystrokes, FPS boost, Zoom).
  2. Download the installer from `cm-pack.pl` and run it.
  3. It automatically creates a profile in your official Minecraft Launcher.
  4. Press Right-Shift in-game to open the CM Client GUI and toggle your preferred PvP mods.

Top Modifications

These are the absolute best mods to increase your FPS and optimize your PvP experience. All links go directly to Modrinth.

Sodium 1.21.x

Feature: Completely replaces the Minecraft rendering engine. Fixes massive lag spikes and can easily double or triple your FPS compared to vanilla.

Lithium 1.21.11

Feature: A general-purpose server and client optimization mod. It improves game physics, chunk loading, and AI calculations without changing any vanilla mechanics.

Entity Culling 1.21.x

Feature: Uses async path-tracing to skip rendering blocks and entities (like chests or players) that you can't currently see behind walls. Huge CPU saver.

Low On Fire Pack

Feature: A resource pack that lowers the fire animation on your screen. Crucial for PvP so your vision isn't blocked when an enemy sets you on fire.
