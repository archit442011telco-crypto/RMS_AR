Mojo Launcher Tutorial

  1. Installation: Download Mojo Launcher from the Play Store or GitHub.
  2. Login: Use your Microsoft account to log in securely.
  3. Java Settings: Go to Settings > Java Tweaks. Set "Memory" to roughly 50% of your phone's total RAM.
  4. Renderer: Select "Holy GL4ES" for the best FPS on newer Android versions.
  5. Versions: Click "Add New" and pick 1.8.9 for PvP or 1.21.1 for survival.

PC PvP Pro Tips

  • W-Tapping: Tap 'W' immediately after hitting an enemy to reset your sprint. This increases knockback.
  • Strafing: Use 'A' and 'D' keys to circle your opponent, making you harder to hit.
  • Jitter/Butterfly: High CPS (12+) helps reduce the knockback you take.

Mojo (Phone) PvP

  • Claw Grip: Use 4 fingers. Left thumb for movement, right thumb for aim, index fingers for attack/jump.
  • Sprint Button: Map a dedicated button for "Sprint" in the custom controls for easier comboing.
  • Sensitivity: Keep sensitivity lower on mobile to ensure your aim doesn't shake during clicking.
